Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect your name, email, and phone number when you register for an account. We also store the data you input into the Service (e.g., patient records, inventory) solely for the purpose of providing the Service.

We use your information strictly to operate the platform, process billing, and verify your identity. We do not sell your personal data or your patients' data to third parties.

Your data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS. We utilize secure MongoDB Atlas clusters to ensure data segregation and protection.
